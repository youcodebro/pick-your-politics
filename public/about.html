PYP

About PYP: Pick Your Politics

PYP is a free political questionnaire that shows users a percentage-based party mix across five alignments based on 64 questions.

PYP exists for people who want to understand their political views without being reduced to one party label. The app asks neutral questions across issue areas such as the economy, healthcare, immigration, climate, education, privacy, justice, and foreign policy.

Neutrality Stance

PYP does not endorse a political party or tell users what to believe. It presents a mix across Democrat, Republican, Libertarian, Green, and Independent alignments so users can see where their answers cluster and where they do not fit neatly.

What Users Get

  • A percentage-based party mix instead of a single label.
  • An issue-by-issue breakdown of where their views land.
  • A shareable results page that keeps detailed insights limited for public viewers.
