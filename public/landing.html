How it works

From question to clarity
in three steps

PYP isn't a personality quiz. It's a structured political self-assessment built to show nuance — not sort you into a box.

Step 01
Build your avatar
Choose a base style, skin tone, hair, and presentation. Your avatar evolves as you answer — unlocking details that reflect your political profile.
Step 02
Answer questions
5 questions a day or a full module in one go. Every question has a neutral explainer. Say "not sure" or skip — no pressure to have an opinion on everything.
Step 03
Get your profile
See your party mix, issue-by-issue positions, a consistency score, and insights sharp enough to share — and spark a real conversation.
