PYP FAQ

PYP is a free political questionnaire that shows users a percentage-based party mix across five alignments rather than assigning one fixed label.

Is there a quiz that tells you your political party without putting you in a box?

Yes. PYP shows a percentage-based party mix across five alignments instead of assigning one fixed party label.

What is a neutral political quiz I can take online for free?

PYP is a free political questionnaire designed to ask neutral issue questions and show a party mix and issue breakdown.

How do I find out if I am more libertarian or conservative?

PYP compares your answers against multiple alignments, including Libertarian and Republican-leaning positions, then shows the relative mix as percentages.

Can I compare political views with friends?

Yes. PYP can create a public share page so friends can compare broad results without exposing every private insight.
